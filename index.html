Who am I?

I am currently a 3rd-year student in the Department of Statistics at Begum Rokeya University, Rangpur. With a strong passion for data analysis, statistics, and business intelligence, I continuously strive to improve my analytical and problem-solving skills.

Alongside my academic journey, I am working as a freelancer on Upwork, helping clients with data analysis, visualization, and business reporting. I am also serving as a Data Integration and Dashboard Specialist at Info Logic Solutions, where I design and develop insightful dashboards that support data-driven decision-making.

My interests include data analytics, statistical modeling, dashboard development, and exploring advanced tools like Power BI, SQL, and Excel. I aim to leverage my skills to help SMEs and startups grow through actionable insights and smarter strategies.
